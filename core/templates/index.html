Our 11-Step Acquisition SOP

We follow a rigid timeline-based process to ensure bankable land titles for solar SPVs.

  • Phase I: Identification & MOU

    From T+0 to T+18 days, land is finalized and basic MOUs are executed.

  • Phase II: Legal Due Diligence

    Document collection leads to the Prima Facie TSR, completed by T+68 days.

  • Phase III: Lease & Possession

    ATL/GPA execution followed by stone marking and physical possession at T+135 days.
